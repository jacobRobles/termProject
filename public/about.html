About Cascadia

Who We Are
Founded in 2025, Cascadia is a premium outdoor gear brand focused on sustainability, comfort, and clean design. We create minimalist, long-lasting gear for people who love the outdoors.
Our Mission
Our goal is to connect people to nature through high-quality, thoughtfully crafted essentials that are built for any adventure.
Why Cascadia?
We believe gear should be functional, beautiful, and respectful of the planet. Cascadia reflects our love for rugged landscapes, open trails, and the spirit of exploration.
